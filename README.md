# Airline Strategy Capstone Project

## Overview
This project explores the **factors determining the success and failure of the airline industry in India**.  
It combines literature review, text analytics, and extended business analytics (forecasting, KPIs, pricing, route optimization) to provide recommendations for upcoming airlines such as **Akasa** and **Air India**.

---

## Role & Context
- MBA Business Analytics Capstone Project  
- Conducted as part of Christ University program  
- Focused on applying **analytics** to evaluate sustainability in the Indian airline sector

---

## Approach
- **Domain Research**: Literature review on Indian aviation growth, taxation, infrastructure, and regulations  
- **Text Analytics**: Extracted insights from news articles, reports, and case studies  
- **Case Studies**: Failures (Jet Airways, Kingfisher) and successes (Indigo, Ryanair, Southwest)  
- **Extended Analytics**: Demand forecasting, KPI dashboards, pricing analysis, route optimization

## Key Findings
- High taxation and poor infrastructure drive costs  
- Leadership failures (Jet Airways) highlight governance risks  
- Currency depreciation and rigid regulations strain profitability  
- New entrants must balance **pricing, route strategy, and operational KPIs** to sustain growth
